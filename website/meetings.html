Meetings

BCLUG is in its beginning stages and planning to hold virtual meetups every two weeks. As our community grows, we look forward to transitioning to in-person events. Our online events will cover a wide range of topics, including interactive workshops, insightful presentations, and lively discussions that cater to various interests and skill levels.

For more information about our upcoming meetings and how to join, please contact us.
